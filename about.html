About Us

Baru Granites is a trusted name in the stone industry, known for delivering premium granite and marble for all kinds of spaces — from cozy homes to grand commercial structures.

With over a decade of hands-on industry experience, we pride ourselves on offering products that combine lasting strength, elegant design, and top-notch finish. Every stone we supply is carefully selected and processed to meet the highest quality standards.

Our expert team works closely with architects, interior designers, and homeowners to provide custom solutions tailored to each project’s needs. At Baru Granites, it’s not just about selling stone — it’s about delivering excellence and building lasting relationships.

We invite you to visit our factory and explore our wide range of natural stone varieties. Let us help you transform your ideas into beautiful, enduring realities.
